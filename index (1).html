Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our POS software

What is POS software?

POS (Point of Sale) software helps businesses process sales transactions, manage inventory, track customer purchases, and generate reports. Our software is specifically designed for small businesses in the Philippines, with features tailored to local needs like sari-sari stores, retail shops, and small groceries.

Is it compatible with my printer or store type?

Our software works with most standard thermal receipt printers commonly used in Philippine businesses (EPSON, Citizen, Bixolon, etc.). It's designed for various store types including sari-sari stores, retail shops, small groceries, boutiques, and other small businesses. If you're unsure about compatibility, you can contact us with your printer model before ordering.

What's the difference between software-only and full POS package?

Our ₱16,990 offer is for the software license and flash drive with installer. This is perfect if you already have a computer and printer. Full POS packages that include hardware (computer, touchscreen, cash drawer, etc.) typically cost ₱50,000 or more. We focus on providing affordable software for businesses that already have basic equipment.

Do I need internet to use the POS software?

Internet is only required for the initial software activation and if you choose to use cloud backup features. Daily operations like sales transactions and inventory management work offline. You'll need internet again if you need remote support from our team.

What if I need help after installation?

We provide free remote support via Zoom or Messenger for 30 days after installation. After that period, you can still contact us for assistance, though priority support may require a small fee. Most users find our instructional videos and documentation sufficient for ongoing use.
